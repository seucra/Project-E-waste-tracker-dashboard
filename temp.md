## Instructions {#sheet-2}

  State E-Waste Management Tracker - Scoring Methodology                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
  -------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ----------------------------------- ------------ ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- -- -- --
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                           A 100-point scorecard for monitoring state-level e-waste generation, collection, formal treatment, compliance, and health/environmental safeguards, in line with the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022 (CPCB, India).                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                           **\#**                                                                                                                                                                                                                 **Category**                        **Weight**   **Indicators (data source sheet)**                                                                                                        
                                                           *1.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Generation & Inventory              *15.0*       District reporting completeness; category-wise breakup; annual inventory update -\> \'1_Generation_Inventory\'                            
                                                           *2.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Collection Performance              *20.0*       Collection rate (%); collection centers per million population; EPR collection-target achievement -\> \'2_Collection_Log\'                
                                                           *3.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Formal Recycling & Treatment        *20.0*       \% processed by authorized recyclers; installed capacity vs generation; material recovery rate -\> \'3_Recycling_Infrastructure\'         
                                                           *4.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Regulatory Compliance               *15.0*       \% producers/EPR entities compliant; inspections conducted vs planned; penalties resolved -\> \'4_Compliance_Tracker\'                    
                                                           *5.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Informal Sector Integration         *10.0*       \% hotspots formalized/remediated; % informal workers registered/trained -\> \'5_Informal_Sector_Hotspots\'                               
                                                           *6.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Environmental & Health Safeguards   *10.0*       \% hotspots environmentally monitored; health screening camps conducted -\> \'6_Env_Health_Monitoring\'                                   
                                                           *7.0*                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Awareness & Outreach                *10.0*       Awareness campaigns vs target; collection drives vs target -\> \'7_Awareness_Programs\'                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  TOTAL                               100.0                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                           *How to use this workbook*                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                           \- Enter data in the 7 numbered sheets (yellow cells = your input). Each sheet has one grey example row - replace/delete it.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                           \- Set your benchmarks/targets on the \'0_Scorecard\' sheet (yellow cells) - e.g. collection centers per million population, EPR targets, inspection/campaign targets.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                           \- The \'0_Scorecard\' sheet pulls live figures from all 7 sheets and computes the state\'s score out of 100 automatically. Do not overwrite formula cells.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                           \- Score bands (customisable on Scorecard): 85-100 Excellent \| 65-84 Good \| 45-64 Needs Improvement \| Below 45 Critical.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                           \- Re-run this tracker every quarter/year to monitor trend - copy the Scorecard total into a tracking log for year-on-year comparison.
